SAVE TIME WITH CREATIVE COOKING

Enjoy a tasty dish in a short time, Jan 27, 2022

Creative Cooking

When we all get home at the end of a long day, the last thing we want to do is cook a delicious meal. So we all like to have delicious recipes that can be cooked effortlessly in a very short time.

In this article we are going to present you about some such fast but healthy and amazing recipes for you and your family.

  1. For using up veggies, a stir-fry is a great solution.

    Whether you’ve got classic ingredients like broccoli and carrots, or any of the veggies used daily, these will all go beautifully in a stir-fry. This is also perfect for using up any leftover meat. Not only is it a delicious, healthy option, but it also takes minutes to make! In fact – you can do almost everything in one pot, wok, or pan, saving on the washing up.

  2. A hash is another brilliant way to use up your veg.

    If you’ve got leftover veggies from last night’s dinner, fry them off in a pan and pop them in the oven with some leftover roast potatoes. Top tip: you can also add tinned goods, a great way to use up tinned food at the back of your cupboard. Make your hash even tastier by adding a fried egg on top and personalise your hash to your taste, why not get creative and serve with those sauces, chutneys and pickles we stash in the fridge! A hash is delicious, super simple, and takes barely any time at all.

The brilliant thing about these food recipes is that they can work with whatever you’ve got available. It means more variety – they’ll taste different every time! – and your food won’t go to waste.
